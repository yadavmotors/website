# Project Transfer Instructions

**For: Transferring Yadav Motors website to Rama's Manus account**

---

## 📋 Before Transfer

### Rama's Checklist

- [ ] Created a Manus account at https://manus.im
- [ ] Verified email address
- [ ] Logged in successfully
- [ ] Ready to receive project

### Your Checklist

- [ ] Have Rama's Manus email address

## 🔄 Transfer Process (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Get Rama's Email

Ask Rama to provide the **exact email address** he used to create his Manus account.

**Example:** rama@email.com

---

### Step 2: Open Project Settings

1. Go to **https://manus.im**
2. Log in with your account
3. Find the **"Yadav Motors"** project
4. Click **"View"** to open it
5. In the top-right, click the **Settings icon** (⚙️)

---

### Step 3: Find Transfer Option

In the Settings panel, look for:
- **"Transfer Ownership"** or
- **"Project Sharing"** or
- **"Collaborators"** or
- **"Team"**

*(The exact name may vary depending on your Manus version)*

---

### Step 4: Initiate Transfer

1. Click the transfer/sharing option
2. Select **"Transfer Ownership"** (not just "Add Collaborator")
3. Enter **Rama's email address**
4. Click **"Send Invitation"** or **"Transfer"**

---

### Step 5: Rama Accepts

Rama will receive an email invitation:
1. He opens the email from Manus
2. Clicks **"Accept"** or **"View Invitation"**
3. Confirms he wants to accept ownership
4. **Done!** He's now the owner

---

## 🤝 Optional: Stay Involved

If you want to help Rama with updates:

**Rama can add you as a Collaborator:**

1. Rama goes to **Settings** (⚙️)
2. Looks for **"Collaborators"** or **"Team"**
3. Clicks **"+ Add Collaborator"**
4. Enters your email
5. Selects your permission level:
   - **View only** — You can see but not edit
   - **Edit** — You can make changes
6. Sends invitation
7. You accept

---

## 📞 Support During Transfer

### If Transfer Fails

1. Check Rama's email address is correct
2. Verify Rama's account is active (he's logged in before)
3. Try again after 5 minutes
4. Contact Manus support: **https://help.manus.im**

### If Rama Doesn't Receive Invitation

1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Ask Rama to log into Manus and check notifications
3. Try sending invitation again
4. Contact Manus support

### If You Need to Undo Transfer

Contact Manus support immediately — they may be able to reverse it within a short time window.
