## Option A — Industrial Precision (Probability: 0.07)

**Design Movement:** Industrial Modernism meets Automotive Engineering

**Core Principles:**
1. Precision over decoration — every element earns its place
2. Dark, workshop-floor atmosphere with high-contrast callouts
3. Asymmetric layouts that feel like technical blueprints
4. Trust is communicated through structured, no-nonsense typography

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#1A3A8F) as the dominant background, white text for clarity, red (#CC2229) used sparingly as a precision accent on CTAs and key stats only. Dark charcoal sections break up the page. The palette evokes a professional workshop — serious, capable, dependable.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed dark hero with a diagonal cut separating sections. Left-anchored text blocks with right-side imagery. Stats bar running horizontally across the page. Services displayed in a tight grid with red accent borders.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal section dividers (clip-path cuts at 3–5 degrees)
- Thin red horizontal rule lines as section separators
- Monospaced numerals for stats (130 reviews, 4.9 stars, 2400 customers)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Subtle hover lifts on service cards. CTA buttons have a red underline sweep on hover. Smooth scroll with section fade-ins.

**Animation:** Entrance animations on scroll using framer-motion — stats count up on viewport entry. Hero headline slides in from left. Section images fade in with slight upward drift.

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Barlow Condensed Bold (automotive, strong, space-efficient)
- Body: DM Sans Regular (readable, modern, approachable)
- Accent numerals: Roboto Mono (precision feel for stats)

## Option B — Trusted Local Expert (Probability: 0.08)

**Design Movement:** Clean Professional with Warm Local Character

**Core Principles:**
1. Light, airy sections that feel trustworthy and approachable
2. Brand blue as a strong structural anchor (nav, CTAs, section headers)
3. Red used only for urgency and primary CTAs
4. Generous whitespace signals confidence and transparency

**Color Philosophy:** White and light grey (#F7F8FA) backgrounds dominate, creating a clean, clinical trustworthiness. Navy blue (#1A3A8F) anchors headers, nav, and footer. Red (#CC2229) is reserved for primary CTA buttons and star ratings. The palette communicates: "We are organised, professional, and have nothing to hide."

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width navy hero with white headline text. Alternating light/dark sections as the page scrolls. Services in a 3-column card grid. Testimonials in a horizontal scroll carousel. Map section with a navy sidebar containing contact details.

**Signature Elements:**
- Navy top navigation with logo left-aligned and phone number right-aligned (always visible)
- Red CTA buttons with white text throughout
- Star rating display in gold/amber with review count

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states on cards lift with a subtle navy shadow. Phone number pulses gently on mobile. Booking button is sticky on mobile scroll.

**Animation:** Clean fade-up on scroll for content sections. Hero text appears with a staggered word-by-word entrance. Service icons rotate 10 degrees on hover.

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Oswald SemiBold (strong, automotive heritage)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (highly legible, professional)
- Nav & labels: Oswald Regular

## Option C — Bold Trust Signal (Probability: 0.09)

**Design Movement:** High-Impact Service Brand — Bold Typography + Photography

**Core Principles:**
1. Large, confident typography does the heavy lifting
2. Real photography (generated) of workshop/cars creates authenticity
3. Blue and red used in full-bleed blocks for maximum brand impact
4. The hero section makes an immediate, visceral trust statement

**Color Philosophy:** Alternating full-bleed sections: navy blue (#1A3A8F), white, and dark charcoal (#1C1C1E). Red (#CC2229) used for the primary CTA and the "Book Now" sticky bar. The rhythm of dark-light-dark sections creates visual energy and keeps the reader scrolling.

**Layout Paradigm:** Oversized hero with a full-bleed workshop image overlay and large headline. Problem/solution section uses a two-column split (dark left, light right). Services in a horizontal scrolling strip. Testimonials in a staggered masonry layout. Footer is full navy with all contact info prominent.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) in faint navy as background texture
- Red sticky "Book Now" bar that appears after scrolling past the hero
- Workshop photography with a subtle blue colour overlay at 20% opacity

**Interaction Philosophy:** The sticky booking bar follows the user down the page. Testimonial cards expand on click to show full review text. Service items have a red underline that sweeps in on hover.

**Animation:** Hero image has a slow Ken Burns zoom. Section numbers count up as they enter the viewport. Testimonial cards stagger in with a 100ms delay between each.

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Bebas Neue (bold, automotive, commanding)
- Sub-headlines: Barlow SemiBold
- Body: Inter (familiar, legible)
- CTA buttons: Barlow Condensed Bold uppercase

## Selected Design: **Option B — Trusted Local Expert**

This approach best serves the StoryBrand goal: the customer (recently moved to Werribee, seeking trust) needs to feel immediately at ease. The clean, light layout with strong navy and red brand anchors communicates professionalism and transparency without feeling intimidating. The alternating section rhythm keeps the StoryBrand narrative flowing naturally from problem to solution to CTA.
